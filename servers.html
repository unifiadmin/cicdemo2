Server Installation and Maintenance Services

Why do you need a server?

When your business gets to a certain size you are going to find it difficult to manage lots of computers individually. Each computer needs to have users created on them, email setup on them, programs and printers installed individually. All this takes time end effort to manage and repeat many of the same things on each of these computers. It all gets more difficult if your users want to be able to move between the computers you have regularly. You will often find managing files that need to be shared between your users becomes a very difficult proposition never mind ensuring the data you have spread out over these machines is protected and backed up correctly.

Installation Service

If these are problems you are beginning to experience then it is reaching the point where you need centralised management and control of your users, computers and data. We can help you by offering you and your business a number of different levels of server setup. From a simple linux based server through to enterprise level server and collaboration solutions. We have experience in various Microsoft products including Windows servers, Exchange email servers, SQL database servers and Sharepoint collaboration services. We can also provide you with reduced costs and less downtime by using virtualisation solutions to implement the server solutions for you. These are all designed to enable you to work with peace of mind and with less problems. If you would like to discuss a custom solution for your business feel free to contact us and we can arrange a survey and provide you with a suggestion that suits your situation.

Maintenance Service

If you already have a fully functioning network and server setup but would like help to maintain and develope your current solution then we can offer maintenance services that can be tailored to your situation. This includes regular proactive maintenance of your servers as well as reactive maintenance to quickly and effectively resolve any issues an problems that may appear. There are a number of different levels of maintenance that can be provided based on your requirements and financial situation. Please feel free to contact us and we can arrange a survey and discuss with you what services we can offer you.
