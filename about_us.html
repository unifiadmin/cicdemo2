About Us
At Capital IT Consultants we are committed to provide an efficient service that we are proud of. Our aim is to make working with computers easier for all our customers. From the simplest questions that only take a few minutes to answer to discussing more complex network environments. From the home user with a single computer to the business with large numbers of users. The company was founded on the principle of always giving the best advice for the customer not necessarily the most expensive advice.
In order to help you we offer a large number of services that make it easier to deal with any problems or requests you may have. Most of our support will begin with a simple phone call and from there we can tailor our services to your needs. To get a more detailed idea of the types of services we can offer please feel free to visit our services page. However please remember if you feel your needs do not fit into the services we offer, as consultants we are willing to listen to your problems and we may be able to tailor a solution that meets your needs.
Thanks to our partnership with the Capital Cartridges retail chain you are able to drop off and pick up from selected capital cartridge branches. Please feel free to visit our partnership page in order to find directions to each of these branches and more details about the services we can offer through these outlets.
You can find all the details on our contact page if you would like to discuss your needs with us.
