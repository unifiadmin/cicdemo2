Introduction
Capital IT Consultants has formed a partnership with Capital Cartridges in order to provide a better service to all our customers and making it easier to find a location nearby. We can offer some of our services at these selected locations spread throughout the city, at a later stage the remaining Capital Cartridges branches not listed here will also be able to offer our services. From the Capital Cartridges branches it will be possible to drop off computers and other equipment that either requires repair or to be upgraded as well as offering data recovery. Staff at each of the branches will be happy to book in your equipment and these items will be transported to our main workshop where our engineers will complete the work you have requested and organise for the equipment to be returned to the branch again for you to pick it up and you will be notified of it having been completed. If at any stage you would like to discuss these services feel free to contact us using one of the methods listed on the contact us page.
