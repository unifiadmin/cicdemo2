Recovery Services
It is very frustrating and difficult to deal with when you lose access to your data. You may be working on an assignment and have accidentally deleted the file, you may have a computer or server that does not boot anymore.These all mean you no longer have the ability to be able to use your data and it usually happens when you really need your data. These things do happen unfortunately so we offer advice and services to retrieve the lost or damaged data, this may be a simple data recovery within our workshop or a more indepth data recovery in some difficult cases using our data recovery partner. The costs of these data recovery solutions can vary greatly depending on the data loss you have suffered, our engineers will be able to advise you on this when you contact us.
