Networking Services
Setting up a network is very important these days and having an internet connection has become another utility in the same way as gas and electricity. We offer a number of network services, from designing, cabling, fault finding, and upgrading existing networks both in the home and your business. Be it either a cabled network or just a secure wifi installation. Please be aware if you having wifi installed it is essential that you consider making it secure or people may be able to see what you are doing, most wifi routers supplied by your internet provider will be supplied setup insecurely. It is also more important these days to secure your internet connection if you use it for busniess and prevent your network being the victim of attacks, be they spam attacks, hacking attempts or other malicious activities, we provide network defence devices to help as well as configuring your internet connection firewalls correctly. We can advise and help with broadband installation and faults you may experience.
