Repair Services
Computers can have problems and it usually happens at a bad or unexpected time. We can offer a comprehensive computer repair service. You can choose to either have onsite support and have an engineer visit your home or business to repair your computer or drop your computer off at one of our repair drop off points through the Capital Cartridges chain. For drop off locations visit our partner page. However please remember that not all repairs can be completed onsite an may require to be taken to our workshop to be fully repaired however our engineers will discuss this with you when you arrange for the repair. We will do our best to get you up and running again as quickly as possible.
